Our Mission

Paris Young Professionals is a social & networking group dedicated to providing a fun and interactive environment for the Paris area young professionals to develop professionally and build a better community.

Initiatives

Professional Networking — Network with local professionals and create new friendships.

Supporting Local Industry's Recruiting Efforts — Help local businesses find great, professional employees.

Leadership Development — Helping young professionals become leaders.

Community Engagement & Development — Getting involved in the community to provide a better living and working experience.
